Example 1:

Task Description:
Balance whitewash paint work is pending on the ceiling in some portions of the OPD building first slab, around installed HVAC ducts, fire-fighting pipelines, and electrical conduits.

Why 1: Why was white washing balance?
Response 1: Slab deshuttering was not completed.

Why 2: Why was deshuttering not completed?
Response 2: Manpower was not available.

Why 3: Why were services laid without whitewash?
Response 3: Lack of coordination.

Why 4: Why was there lack of coordination?
Response 4: The civil and services teams did not align their work sequence before starting service installation.

Why 5: Why was the work sequence not aligned?
Response 5: There was no proper pre-activity coordination and clearance process before allowing service installation.

Root Cause: Services were installed before completing whitewash due to poor coordination and lack of work-front clearance.

Corrective Action: Complete whitewashing in the left-out areas and ensure service installation starts only after civil finishing clearance.


Example 2:

Task Description:
Honeycomb observed in column.

Why 1: Why was honeycombing observed in the column?
Response 1: The concrete mix did not flow uniformly during pouring.

Why 2: Why did the concrete mix not flow uniformly?
Response 2: Inadequate vibration was done during the concreting process.

Why 3: Why was the vibration inadequate?
Response 3: The vibrator was not applied at the correct intervals and required depth.

Why 4: Why was the vibrator not applied correctly?
Response 4: The concreting team did not follow the approved method statement and checklist.

Why 5: Why was the approved method statement not followed?
Response 5: Supervision and monitoring during the critical concreting activity were insufficient.

Root Cause: Honeycombing occurred due to improper vibration and insufficient supervision during concreting.

Corrective Action: Repair the honeycombed area using approved methods and ensure strict supervision with checklist-based monitoring during future concreting.


Example 3:

Task Description:
Paint applied without primer.

Why 1: Why was paint applied without primer?
Response 1: The primer application step was skipped before painting.

Why 2: Why was the primer application step skipped?
Response 2: Workers were not clearly informed about the surface preparation sequence.

Why 3: Why were workers not informed?
Response 3: The site supervisor did not verify the painting sequence before starting the activity.

Why 4: Why did the supervisor not verify the sequence?
Response 4: The supervisor was not regularly available at the work location.

Why 5: Why was the supervisor not regularly available?
Response 5: A dedicated supervisor was not assigned for the painting activity.

Root Cause: Paint was applied without primer due to lack of dedicated supervision and missed verification of surface preparation requirements.

Corrective Action: Remove the affected paint, apply primer before repainting, and assign dedicated supervision for painting activities.
